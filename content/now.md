## What I'm Working On

### Silicon Tapeout — BIO Coprocessor

This is the big one. I'm neck-deep into a personal silicon tapeout project: a 2-core BIO coprocessor derived from the BIO/baochip-1x IP [link](https://baochip.com/), on GF180MCU through the wafer.space Run 2. I'm collaborating with bunnie Huang, who's funding the slot. 

Its a Hail-Mary attempt as the hard deadline for GDS is June 30. and I have just finished setting up the tools as on 9 april,2026.

This has been a long-term goal of mine — going from FPGA to actual silicon — and it's finally happening. I mean I have worked previously on the tiny tapeout but just the rtl side.

### Vicharak — ASIC/FPGA

By day, I'm ASIC/FPGA engineer at Vicharak, working on FPGA-based products. My main focus on a model agnostic ML accelerator design but most of it comes under NDA so. 

Other then that I am maintainer and contributor for Shrike and Shrike-Lite boards (RP2040/RP2350 + FPGA). Recent work on porting it from RP2040 to ESP32 with dual-architecture support for Shrike-Fi , and writing documentation in MyST/Sphinx-design. 

I also recently spent time debugging LPDDR4x memory interface issues on a TI180J484 FPGA board — the kind of deep hardware bring-up work I find most satisfying. Its working now.
